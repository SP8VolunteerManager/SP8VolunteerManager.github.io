SP8 Volunteer Manager

Achieve More With Less

Overview

Many organizations relying on volunteer efforts still utilize traditional, manual methods for managing volunteer data, primarily through mail-in audits. This process is often time-consuming and repetitive, hindering the efficiency of volunteer management.

The SP8 Volunteer Manager app is designed to streamline and automate the management of volunteer data for organizations. This web application serves as a platform where volunteers can submit their information directly to the organization they have worked for, including details like name, phone number, email, times, and tasks completed. This data is then sent to the volunteer manager within the organization for approval.

Key Features

Volunteer Submission
Manager Approval System
Database Management
Volunteers can search for their respective organizations and submit their details directly through the app.
Volunteer managers receive notifications for submissions, which they can approve or deny. Approved data is automatically updated in the PostgreSQL database.
The app allows managers to view, manage, and export their volunteer database, including exporting data to Excel for easy sharing and reporting.

Objectives

  • Reduce Repetitive
  • Simplify the Process
  • Manage Volunteers
  • Enhance Database Management
